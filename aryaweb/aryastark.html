ARYA STARK of House Winterfell
The third child and second daughter of Lord Eddard Stark and his wife, Lady Catelyn Stark.
Trained as a Faceless Man at the House of Black and White in Braavos. Became no one, then abandoned that to reclaim her identity as a Stark and bring to justice those who wronged her family.
Sister to Robb Stark, Sansa Stark, Bran Stark, Rickon Stark, and a "bastard half-brother", Jon Snow.
A badass in general, and a great reason to finally watch Game of Thrones.
