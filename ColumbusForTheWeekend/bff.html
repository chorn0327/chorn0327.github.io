Best Friend

Friday

Every proper Friday evening with a best friend starts at your favorite watering hole. I would recommend Out-R-Inn for the real dive bar experience, complete with pool tables and neon.

Don't forget to grab a piece of pizza from Cicilia's next door.

Saturday

Mornings are for coffee and contemplation. Therefore, you'd better get yourself to Fox in the Snow for plenty of both as they don't offer wifi. They do offer the best breakfast sandwich in Columbus though so it's a good trade off.

When the weather's nice, find yourself a farmers market to wander around. Support your local community!

If manicures are up your alley, there's not better place in town than Laquer Gallery.

Stop by Sweet Carrot for a late lunch. I'd recommend the mac and cheese with deep fried artichoke hearts - yum.

The Old North has several thrift stores to shop at, you're certain to find a funky new addition to your wardrobe while wandering around.

Sunday

Union Cafe in the Short North often has Drag Brunch - a brunch buffet featuring a rotating cast of Drag Queens who will perform during your meal. Make sure to bring singles to tip the lovely ladies.

A good way to end a lovely visit with a friend is to take them for a walk around campus and show them your favorite haunts. I recommend the KSA and Thompson - they're crowd pleasers. Show off where you come from, you should be proud of it! And you never know what you might find.
