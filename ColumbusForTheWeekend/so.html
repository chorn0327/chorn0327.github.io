Significant Other

Friday

A great way to start off your weekend is with some live music. Columbus has no shortage of venues to check out a show. The Schottenstein Center - where OSU Basketball plays - often showcases touring artists and you can grab a ticket at Dtix at a discount too.

At this point, you'll probably be hungry for some good eats. Swinging by Hot Chicken Takeover in Clintonville is a popular choice. You get to pick the spice level for the chicken and the mac and cheese is insane.

Saturday

Kafe Kerouac is a great lowkey choice to grab a cup of coffee right by campus. They have board games you can borrow to play and a library's worth of books to peruse. The vibe is casual so it's a nice place to swing by, especially if you have a little bit of homework to do before spending the rest of the weekend sight seeing.

Believe it or not, but Ohio State has several collections housed on campus that many students aren't aware of. One of the best is the Billy Ireland Cartoon Museum, which archives over 300,000 original comics.

Sushi Time has a great all you can eat lunch special for those who like sushi. They also offer tempura and soup to round out the meal. Be careful not to have eyes bigger than your stomach as to not waste food.

Every first Saturday evening, The Short North galleries open their doors for a gallery hop. This is a great chance to see some of the amazing art being created here in Columbus. It's fun to walk around and discuss your favorite art pieces, maybe grabbing a drink or two along the way.

If you're already in the Short North, one of the most interesting places to grab a drink is Oddfellows Liquor Bar. With a rotating selection of cocktails (including slushies) you will absolutely find something new to try.

Sunday

Mornings are for coffee and contemplation. Therefore, you'd better get yourself to Fox in the Snow for plenty of both as they don't offer wifi. They do offer the best breakfast sandwich in Columbus though so it's a good trade off.

Even walking around the neighborhood you live in is fun when you're in good company. Show off where you come from, you should be proud of it! And you never know what you might find.
